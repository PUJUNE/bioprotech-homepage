Since 2000, Bioprotech has built every product around a single standard — the safety of clinicians and patients. The record speaks in numbers.

Desde su fundación en 2000, Bioprotech fabrica cada producto en torno a un único estándar: la seguridad de los profesionales sanitarios y los pacientes. Los resultados hablan en cifras.

의료용 바이오센서 제조 전문기업, 바이오프로테크는 Bio sensors와 Bio products라는 두 축으로 성장해 왔습니다.

강원도 원주 문막 동화첨단의료기기산업단지에 본사를 두고, 한국(BPH), 미국(BPU), 중국 연태(BPC), 광저우(BPG)의 4개 법인 체계로 운영합니다. ECG Electrode, TENS 전극, ESU Pencil, EEG/EMG 전극까지 수술·진단·재활의 전 영역을 아우릅니다.

A manufacturer of medical biosensors — Bioprotech has grown along two pillars: Bio sensors and Bio products.

Headquartered at the Donghwa Advanced Medical Device Industrial Complex in Wonju, Korea, with four entities — Korea (BPH), USA (BPU), and China (BPC Yantai, BPG Guangzhou). Our portfolio spans surgery, diagnostics, and rehabilitation — ECG, TENS, ESU, EEG/EMG.

Empresa especializada en la fabricación de biosensores médicos, Bioprotech ha crecido sobre dos pilares: Bio sensors y Bio products.

Con sede en el Complejo Industrial de Dispositivos Médicos Avanzados Donghwa, en Wonju (Corea), opera mediante cuatro filiales: Corea (BPH), EE. UU. (BPU) y China (BPC Yantai, BPG Guangzhou). Su gama abarca toda la cirugía, el diagnóstico y la rehabilitación: electrodos ECG, electrodos TENS, ESU Pencil y electrodos EEG/EMG.
